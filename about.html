1st International Conference on Sustainable Energy and AI-Driven Technologies for Next Generation Applications

SEATNGA 2026

April 24 -25, Anurag University, Hyderabad, India (Hybrid Mode)

About the Conference

The International Conference on Renewable Energy, Artificial Intelligence, and Sustainable Transportation serves as a dynamic platform that brings together leading researchers, scientists, engineers, and industry professionals from across the globe. The conference aims to foster knowledge exchange and promote collaboration on the latest innovations and trends shaping the future of clean energy, intelligent systems for next generation applications.

In the face of global challenges such as climate change and resource depletion, renewable energy sources are essential to building a sustainable future. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in revolutionizing energy systems, improving efficiency, predictive maintenance, and decision-making in energy grids and smart technologies. With AI-driven insights, advancements in renewable energy—from solar and wind to hydropower can be optimized, addressing supply-demand challenges, and lowering emissions.

The conference will also focus on sustainable transportation solutions, exploring electric vehicles (EVs), autonomous driving technologies, and smart city infrastructures. It delves into how AI algorithms enhance EV performance, optimize charging infrastructure, and improve traffic management systems, contributing to greener cities.

By bringing together experts in these transformative fields, the conference aspires to foster interdisciplinary discussions, accelerate the development of innovative technologies, and build partnerships that will drive progress in renewable energy, AI, and sustainable transportation. It will be a pivotal event in shaping future research directions and policy-making efforts toward a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable world.

About the Department

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at Anurag University enlightens the students with an academic benchmark that instils in them an electrifying and enriching educational framework. Accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), the EEE department assures a promising future for the students at Anurag University.

The EEE department was conceptualized in 2002 by introducing B. Tech program with current intake of 120. The department has Power Electronics and Electrical Drives (PEED) as PG specialization with an intake of 36 students. The department has been a hive of academic activity and instrumental in creating successful careers for many students. The Department has well equipped laboratories with sophisticated lab equipment. Consistent growth was witnessed in development and consequently the Department was accredited by NBA in 2008, 2017 and reaccredited by NBA in 2022.

The Department has strong industry interaction and signed MoU's with 30 core industries. It offers Internships and Placements in Core Industries through this Interaction with Industry thus bridging the gap between Industry and Academics.
